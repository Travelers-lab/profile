Chengjin Wang

I'm a PhD student in Intelligent Science and Technology at Tongji University.
DHis research interests lie at the intersection of robotics, neuroscience, computer graphics, and machine learning. His goal is to develop novel motion generation methods using multimodal sensing, physical modeling, and end-to-end learning to enhance the predictability of robotic movements during complex physical interactions.

I'm fortunate to be advised by Professor Shenrun Jie and Yanmin Zhou. My research will focus on anticipated motion generation for robots in unstructured environments.
